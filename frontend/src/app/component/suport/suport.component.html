Support

At PG Finder, we are committed to providing dependable and timely support to ensure every resident enjoys a smooth, comfortable, and hassle-free stay. Whether you need help with booking, room facilities, maintenance, food services, or general assistance, our support team is always ready to help.

Booking & Admission Support

Need help with room booking, admission process, or document submission? Our team assists residents with registration, joining formalities, room allocation, and onboarding support.

Maintenance Assistance

Residents can report maintenance issues related to electrical systems, plumbing, cleaning, furniture, Wi-Fi, or room facilities. Our team works to resolve all maintenance concerns quickly and efficiently.

Food & Meal Support

For issues related to meal quality, food timings, or dining services, residents may contact management for immediate assistance and resolution.

Safety & Security Support

Resident safety is our highest priority. Support is available for visitor concerns, room security, locker access, emergency reporting, and safety-related issues.

Complaint Resolution

Residents can raise complaints regarding accommodation services, facilities, hygiene, discipline, or operational issues. Every complaint is reviewed and handled promptly by management.

Emergency Assistance

In urgent situations, residents should contact management immediately for emergency support and quick action.
