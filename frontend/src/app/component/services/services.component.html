Our Services

At PG Finder, we provide a complete and reliable platform designed to make Paying Guest accommodation simple, comfortable, and well-managed. Our services are focused on delivering a safe, convenient, and organized living experience for students and working professionals.

Comfortable Accommodation

We provide clean, secure, and affordable PG rooms with a comfortable environment suitable for students and professionals. Rooms are designed to ensure convenience, privacy, and peaceful living.

Daily Meals Service

Nutritious and hygienic meals are provided to residents with fixed meal schedules for breakfast, lunch, and dinner to ensure timely and healthy food service.

Maintenance & Support

We provide regular maintenance support for electrical, plumbing, cleaning, Wi-Fi, and other essential services to ensure residents enjoy a hassle-free stay.

Safety & Security

Resident safety is our priority. Secure accommodation, locker facilities, visitor guidelines, and disciplined management help maintain a safe and respectful living environment for all residents.

Guest Accommodation

Guest accommodation is available with or without food on a daily charge basis, subject to prior approval and management guidelines.

Professional Management

Our management team ensures smooth day-to-day operations, quick complaint resolution, and clear communication for all accommodation-related services and support.

PG Rules & Guidelines

  • Application form and ID proof submission are mandatory at the time of joining.
  • Monthly rent must be paid on or before the 5th of every month.
  • Residents must inform management at least one month before vacating the PG.
  • Maintenance charges may be deducted from the security deposit during vacating.
  • Guest accommodation is available on prior approval with applicable charges.
  • Residents must inform management before guests or friends visit.
  • Lights, fans, TV, geysers, and appliances must be switched off before leaving.
  • Misbehavior inside or outside the PG premises will not be tolerated.
  • Residents are advised to keep valuables safely locked in provided lockers.
  • Management is not responsible for valuables kept outside lockers.
  • Residents must switch off geysers/heaters before leaving the room.
  • Complaints regarding electrical, plumbing, cleaning, Wi-Fi, or food should be reported immediately.
  • Emergency issues should be reported to management without delay.
  • Use of extra electrical appliances may result in additional charges.
  • Visitors must follow accommodation rules and discipline policies.
